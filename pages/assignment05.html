Assignment 5 - Final Vision

For the final assignment, students need to focus on attracting potential future customers by having a digital marketing design piece that explains your project in detail.

Evaluation Criteria

In order to achieve this, your marketing website should target your audience well.

Thus, this website should explain and have a selling aspect of your project also. Make sure to place an emphasis on:

  • Key core features with a clear explanation
  • What are the benefits/values of your projects
  • Speculative testimonials
  • Speculative "as seen on tv" with publisher logo review
  • Etc

Also, every student should leverage their existing materials done in class such as:

  • High Fidelity Mocks
  • UI Animations
  • Etc

Make sure to check website suggestions at the homepage for inspirations.


Due December 3 - Beginning of the class
